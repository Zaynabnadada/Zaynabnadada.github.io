Assalamu alykum! Hi! My name is Zaynab Nadada, oh yes...just as it says at the top over there! I am the founder of the clothing brand- Zaynab Nadada. I am currently undergoing an MSC degree at Nile University with the aim of furthering my studies in a business field.

About The Company

Zaynab Nadada is a clothing brand, founded in the year 2020. We provide ladies with comfortable yet modest and affordable cloting, inclusive of hijabs, jilbabs, Abayas, etc. Our mission is to create a world where every woman feels comfortable and confident. I receive orders mostly via social media, and I have a display area at Gwarimpa, Abuja.
