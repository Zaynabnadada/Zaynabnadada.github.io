About The Company

Zaynab Nadada is a clothing brand, founded in the year 2020. It is a modest clothing brand with the sole aim of catering for the needs of the female fashion sector in a modest yet affordable and classy manner. We tailor, produce, sell, train and of course, offer our customers that are interested in selling our products wholesale services. Our mission is to create a world where every woman feels comfortable and confident in her modest appearance. We receive orders mostly via social media, precisely via the business's instagram and whatsapp account. We produce Jilbabs, hijabs of different styles namely; the zahra style which is a hijab and a gown, the 2 in 1 which is a knee length hijab, an ankle length skirt and finally the pocket hand hijab, which has a unique design just like a pocket in the hand area. Currently, the business has deleveloped its first internet medium, that is, our business blog and is looking forward to venturing into other services such as the production of niqabs, abayas, arm sleeves, veils, bubus, etc We have attached the pictures of our beautiful and elegant products here(check out our portfolio below, where you can find the pictures)
